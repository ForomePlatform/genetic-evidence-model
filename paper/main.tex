\paragraph{Contributions.}
This paper makes the following contributions:
\begin{itemize}
  \item A domain-analysis-driven semantic model for genetic evidence,
        comprising a small set of core classes
        (\ScientificEvidence, \EvidenceVariable, \EvidenceAssertion)
        and their genetic specializations, together with a compact
        dimensional vocabulary structured by a conditional-activation
        mechanism.
  \item A worked mapping of the model to six published genetic-evidence
        papers spanning human, population, model-organism, and polygenic-score
        genetics, demonstrating coverage across heterogeneous evidence types.
  \item A human--AI collaborative annotation workflow, using the model
        as schema, together with an evaluation of the workflow against
        manually curated ground-truth annotations.
  \item An alignment sketch between the model and existing standards
        (FHIR Evidence, ECO, SEPIO, GA4GH GKS, IAO/OBI, PROV-O).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Core Classes}

We define three abstract classes:

\begin{description}
  \item[\ScientificEvidence] A unit of evidence, typically linked to a
    publication or dataset, described by a set of dimensions
    (\cref{tab:dimensions}).
  \item[\EvidenceVariable] A variable extracted from one or more
    \ScientificEvidence{} items (for example, \emph{gnomAD AF for variant
    V}, or \emph{LOD score in family F}), also annotated by relevant
    dimensions.
  \item[\EvidenceAssertion] A formal statement or predicate over one or
    more \EvidenceVariable{} instances, intended to be machine-checkable
    and composable in decision workflows.
\end{description}

Specialising these to genetics yields \GeneticEvidence, \GeneticEvidenceVariable,
and \GeneticEvidenceAssertion.

\begin{lstlisting}[language=yaml, caption={Conditional activation rule expressed as a SHACL-like constraint (sketch).}, label={lst:shacl}]
# When knowledge_domain is HUMAN_GENETICS and target_type is GENE,
# mode_of_inheritance must be present.
- id: GeneticEvidenceShape_MoI
  targetClass: GeneticEvidence
  condition:
    and:
      - path: knowledge_domain
        hasValue: HUMAN_GENETICS
      - path: target_type
        hasValue: GENE
  requires:
    path: mode_of_inheritance
    minCount: 1
\end{lstlisting}
