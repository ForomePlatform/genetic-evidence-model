\section{Dimension coverage over the annotated corpus}
\label{sec:supp-coverage-note}

This note reports how often each dimension is populated across the 29
\GeneticEvidence{} items in the pilot corpus, and in how many
items each dimension is applicable under the conditional-activation
rules. The counts are computed from the released YAML annotations and
the SHACL shapes, and a machine-readable report is provided in the
repository under
\texttt{annotations/coverage.md}.\footnote{\url{https://github.com/ForomePlatform/genetic-evidence-model/blob/master/annotations/coverage.md}}

The always-required dimensions are 29 of 29 by construction, with one
exception: Knowledge Domain is 28 of 29, because a cited anti-p40
antibody trial is drug-response evidence with no fitting genetic
knowledge domain. Among conditional dimensions, well-calibrated ones
such as Measurement Target (10 of 11) and Organism (8 of 8) are almost
always populated when their activation conditions fire, whereas others
highlight activation or enumeration gaps that motivate candidate
extensions, as discussed in the main text.
